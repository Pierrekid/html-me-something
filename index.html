Champions League

Champions league is Eurpoean futbol competition that takes place annually.

Let the games begin...

The champions league begins soon! Defending champions Real Madrid return to defnd their crown against the powers of europe. Runners up from last year, liverpool try to take another shot of getting the crown. Both of these teams will be facing huge opposition as the the powers of europe gather. Here are some of the favorites...

Return of the prodical reds

Manchester United return for their second run with Jose mourinho to recapture the glory days of United. In recent years, United have struggled to become the team they once were. However under mourinho they have a groove and are primed to make a deep run. With paul pogba, Lukuka and DDG leading the charge, United have all the pieces to make a great champions league run.

Familiar foes, stronger than ever

Barcelona and Lionel messi look to capture yet another champions league. Although disspointed over the summer with the world cup, Messi will be looking to lift his team to glory. WIth a fully fit and eligible countinho supporting, Barcelona are ready to take on their domesttic rivals and european opposition.

New look Ronaldo, and new look Juve

Hard to call them an underdog, but Juventus pose a new kind of threat to contenders in this years champions league. With the addition of Ronaldo to their roster, Juventus just might have found the piece to put them over the top. WIth the likes of Dybala and cuadrado helping out, Many believe Juventus pose the biggest threat to challenging for the title over the rest of the group.
